Let me tell you something about pianos.. the first piano was an acoustic, keyboard and stringed musical instrument where the strings were struck by wooden hammers, coated with a softer material.

The piano was invented in Italy in 1709 by harpsichord maker Bartolomeo di Francesco Cristofori. The word piano is the shortened version of the word pianoforte, which means soft (piano) and loud (forte).
